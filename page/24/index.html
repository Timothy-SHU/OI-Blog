The first number n on such a line is the number of different kinds of products that are part of the offer (1≤n≤5)(1 \le n \le 5)(1≤n≤5). The next n pairs of numbers (c,k)(c,k)(c,k) indicate that kkk items (1≤k≤5)(1 \le k \le 5)(1≤k≤5) with product code ccc (1≤c≤999)(1 \le c \le 999)(1≤c≤999) are involved in the offer. The last number ppp on the line stands for the reduced price (1≤p≤9999)(1 \le p \le 9999)(1≤p≤9999). The reduced price of an offer is less than the sum of the regular prices.
