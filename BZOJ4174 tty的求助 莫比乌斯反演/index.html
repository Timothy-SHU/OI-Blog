BZOJ4174 tty的求助 <莫比乌斯反演>

求∑n=1N∑m=1M∑k=0m−1⌊nk+xm⌋\sum_{n=1}^{N}\sum_{m=1}^{M}\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk+x}{m}\rfloor∑n=1N​∑m=1M​∑k=0m−1​⌊mnk+x​⌋，其中xxx为实数。

第一行仅有两个正整数N,MN,MN,M和一个实数xxx。

输出共一行，由于结果过大，所以请输出上式对998244353998244353998244353取模的结果。

Sample Output

Explanation

当n=1,m=1n=1,m=1n=1,m=1时，sum=1sum=1sum=1
当n=1,m=2n=1,m=2n=1,m=2时，sum=1sum=1sum=1
当n=1,m=3n=1,m=3n=1,m=3时，sum=1sum=1sum=1
当n=2,m=1n=2,m=1n=2,m=1时，sum=1sum=1sum=1

Solution

Ans=∑n=1N∑m=1M∑k=0m−1⌊nk+xm⌋Ans=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\sum_{m=1}^{M}\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk+x}{m}\rfloor\\ Ans=n=1∑N​m=1∑M​k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk+x​⌋

对于最后一次求和：

        ∑k=0m−1⌊nk+xm⌋=∑k=0m−1⌊nk−nk%m+x+nk%mm⌋=∑k=0m−1⌊nk%m+xm+nk−nk%mm⌋=∑k=0m−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋+∑k=0m−1nkm−∑k=0m−1nk%mm\begin{aligned} &\;\;\;\;\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk-nk\%m+x+nk\%m}{m}\rfloor\\ &=\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}+\frac{nk-nk\%m}{m}\rfloor\\ &=\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor+\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\frac{nk}{m}-\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\frac{nk\%m}{m}\\ \end{aligned} ​k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk+x​⌋=k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk−nk%m+x+nk%m​⌋=k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk%m+x​+mnk−nk%m​⌋=k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋+k=0∑m−1​mnk​−k=0∑m−1​mnk%m​​

先考虑前一项：
设d=gcd⁡(n,m)d=\gcd(n,m)d=gcd(n,m)，那么n⋅md≡0mod  mn\cdot\frac{m}{d}\equiv0\mod{m}n⋅dm​≡0modm。于是有n(k+md)%m=nk%m+nmd%m=nk%mn(k+\frac{m}{d})\%m=nk\%m+\frac{nm}{d}\%m=nk\%mn(k+dm​)%m=nk%m+dnm​%m=nk%m，即可知nk%m=n(k%md)%mnk\%m=n(k\%\frac{m}{d})\%mnk%m=n(k%dm​)%m。

        ∑k=0m−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋=∑k=0m−1⌊n(k%md)%m+xm⌋=mmd∑k=0md−1⌊n(k%md)%m+xm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋\begin{aligned} &\;\;\;\;\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{n(k\%\frac{m}{d})\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=\frac{m}{\frac{m}{d}}\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{n(k\%\frac{m}{d})\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ \end{aligned} ​k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋=k=0∑m−1​⌊mn(k%dm​)%m+x​⌋=dm​m​k=0∑dm​−1​⌊mn(k%dm​)%m+x​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋​

对于kkk取遍0d,dd,2dd,⋯ ,m−dd\frac{0}{d},\frac{d}{d},\frac{2d}{d},\cdots,\frac{m-d}{d}d0​,dd​,d2d​,⋯,dm−d​，nknknk的值依次为nd⋅0,nd⋅d,nd⋅2d,⋯ ,nd⋅(md−1)d\frac{n}{d}\cdot 0,\frac{n}{d}\cdot d,\frac{n}{d}\cdot 2d,\cdots,\frac{n}{d}\cdot (\frac{m}{d}-1)ddn​⋅0,dn​⋅d,dn​⋅2d,⋯,dn​⋅(dm​−1)d。由于nd\frac{n}{d}dn​与⌊md⌋\lfloor\frac{m}{d}\rfloor⌊dm​⌋互质，所以nd⋅k%m\frac{n}{d}\cdot k\%mdn​⋅k%m一定取遍0,1,2,⋯ ,(md−1)0,1,2,\cdots,(\frac{m}{d}-1)0,1,2,⋯,(dm​−1)，因而nk%mnk\%mnk%m一定取遍0,d,2d,⋯ ,(md−1)d0,d,2d,\cdots,(\frac{m}{d}-1)d0,d,2d,⋯,(dm​−1)d。故而有

        ∑k=0m−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊kd+xm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊kd+x%m+x−x%mm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊kd+x%mm⌋+d⋅md⋅x−x%mm\begin{aligned} &\;\;\;\;\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{kd+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{kd+x\%m+x-x\%m}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{kd+x\%m}{m}\rfloor+d\cdot\frac{m}{d}\cdot\frac{x-x\%m}{m}\\ \end{aligned} ​k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mkd+x​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mkd+x%m+x−x%m​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mkd+x%m​⌋+d⋅dm​⋅mx−x%m​​

又发现0≤kd+x%m<2m⇒0≤⌊kd+x%mm⌋<20\le kd+x\%m<2m\Rightarrow0\le\lfloor\frac{kd+x\%m}{m}\rfloor<20≤kd+x%m<2m⇒0≤⌊mkd+x%m​⌋<2，故

        ∑k=0m−1⌊nk%m+xm⌋=d∑k=0md−1⌊kd+x%mm⌋+d⋅md⋅x−x%mm=d×(∑k=0md−1[kd≥m−x%m]+x−x%md)=d×(∑k=0md−1[k≥⌈m−x%md⌉]+x−x%md)=d×(⌊x%md⌋+⌊xd⌋−⌊x%md⌋)=d⌊xd⌋\begin{aligned} &\;\;\;\;\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\lfloor\frac{nk\%m+x}{m}\rfloor\\ &=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\lfloor\frac{kd+x\%m}{m}\rfloor+d\cdot\frac{m}{d}\cdot\frac{x-x\%m}{m}\\ &=d\times(\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}[kd\ge m-x\%m]+\frac{x-x\%m}{d})\\ &=d\times(\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}[k\ge\lceil\frac{m-x\%m}{d}\rceil]+\frac{x-x\%m}{d})\\ &=d\times(\lfloor\frac{x\%m}{d}\rfloor+\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor-\lfloor\frac{x\%m}{d}\rfloor)\\ &=d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor\\ \end{aligned} ​k=0∑m−1​⌊mnk%m+x​⌋=dk=0∑dm​−1​⌊mkd+x%m​⌋+d⋅dm​⋅mx−x%m​=d×(k=0∑dm​−1​[kd≥m−x%m]+dx−x%m​)=d×(k=0∑dm​−1​[k≥⌈dm−x%m​⌉]+dx−x%m​)=d×(⌊dx%m​⌋+⌊dx​⌋−⌊dx%m​⌋)=d⌊dx​⌋​

对于后两项：

∑k=0m−1nkm=n⋅m⋅(m−1)2m=nm−n2∑k=0m−1nk%mm=d∑k=0md−1kdm=m−d2\sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\frac{nk}{m}=\frac{n\cdot m\cdot(m-1)}{2m}=\frac{nm-n}{2}\\ \sum_{k=0}^{m-1}\frac{nk\%m}{m}=d\sum_{k=0}^{\frac{m}{d}-1}\frac{kd}{m}=\frac{m-d}{2}\\ k=0∑m−1​mnk​=2mn⋅m⋅(m−1)​=2nm−n​k=0∑m−1​mnk%m​=dk=0∑dm​−1​mkd​=2m−d​

将三项和前面两个求和连起来：

Let  Si=1+2+⋯+i=i×(i+1)2,Ans=∑n=1N∑m=1M(d⌊xd⌋+nm−n−m+d2)=∑n=1N∑m=1M(d⌊xd⌋+d2)+12SNSM−M2SN−N2SM=∑n=1N∑m=1M(d⌊xd⌋+d2)+12SNSM−M2SN−N2SM\begin{aligned} Let\;&S_i=1+2+\cdots+i=\frac{i\times(i+1)}{2},\\ Ans&=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\sum_{m=1}^{M}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{nm-n-m+d}{2})\\ &=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\sum_{m=1}^{M}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{d}{2})+\frac{1}{2}S_NS_M-\frac{M}{2}S_N-\frac{N}{2}S_M\\ &=\sum_{n=1}^{N}\sum_{m=1}^{M}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{d}{2})+\frac{1}{2}S_NS_M-\frac{M}{2}S_N-\frac{N}{2}S_M\\ \end{aligned} LetAns​Si​=1+2+⋯+i=2i×(i+1)​,=n=1∑N​m=1∑M​(d⌊dx​⌋+2nm−n−m+d​)=n=1∑N​m=1∑M​(d⌊dx​⌋+2d​)+21​SN​SM​−2M​SN​−2N​SM​=n=1∑N​m=1∑M​(d⌊dx​⌋+2d​)+21​SN​SM​−2M​SN​−2N​SM​​

对前面的和式做莫比乌斯反演：

        ∑n=1N∑m=1M(d⌊xd⌋+d2)=∑d=1min⁡(N,M)(d⌊xd⌋+d2)∑i=1⌊Nd⌋∑j=1⌊Md⌋[gcd⁡(i,j)=1]=∑d=1min⁡(N,M)(d⌊xd⌋+d2)∑i=1⌊Nd⌋∑j=1⌊Md⌋∑t∣i,jμ(t)=∑d=1min⁡(N,M)(d⌊xd⌋+d2)∑t=1min⁡(⌊Nd⌋,⌊Md⌋)μ(t)⌊Ntd⌋⌊Mtd⌋\begin{aligned} &\;\;\;\;\sum_{n=1}^{N}\sum_{m=1}^{M}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{d}{2})\\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{\min(N,M)}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{d}{2})\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{N}{d}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{M}{d}\rfloor}[\gcd(i,j)=1]\\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{\min(N,M)}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{d}{2})\sum_{i=1}^{\lfloor\frac{N}{d}\rfloor}\sum_{j=1}^{\lfloor\frac{M}{d}\rfloor}\sum_{t|i,j}\mu(t)\\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{\min(N,M)}(d\lfloor\frac{x}{d}\rfloor+\frac{d}{2})\sum_{t=1}^{\min(\lfloor\frac{N}{d}\rfloor,\lfloor\frac{M}{d}\rfloor)}\mu(t)\lfloor\frac{N}{td}\rfloor\lfloor\frac{M}{td}\rfloor\\ \end{aligned} ​n=1∑N​m=1∑M​(d⌊dx​⌋+2d​)=d=1∑min(N,M)​(d⌊dx​⌋+2d​)i=1∑⌊dN​⌋​j=1∑⌊dM​⌋​[gcd(i,j)=1]=d=1∑min(N,M)​(d⌊dx​⌋+2d​)i=1∑⌊dN​⌋​j=1∑⌊dM​⌋​t∣i,j∑​μ(t)=d=1∑min(N,M)​(d⌊dx​⌋+2d​)t=1∑min(⌊dN​⌋,⌊dM​⌋)​μ(t)⌊tdN​⌋⌊tdM​⌋​
