Description

Princess Twilight went to Celestia and Luna’s old castle to research the chest from the Elements of Harmony.

![](https://odzkskevi.qnssl.com/27446e1e5b6e7b1f3e93c237f646aae1?v=1512139280)

A sequence of positive integers bib_ibi​ is harmony if and only if for every two elements of the sequence their greatest common divisor equals 111. According to an ancient book, the key of the chest is a harmony sequence bi which minimizes the following expression:

∑i=1n∣ai−bi∣\sum_{i=1}^{n}{|a_i-b_i|} i=1∑n​∣ai​−bi​∣

You are given sequence aia_iai​, help Princess Twilight to find the key.
