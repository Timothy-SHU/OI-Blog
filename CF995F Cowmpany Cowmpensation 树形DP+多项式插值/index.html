Description

Allen, having graduated from the MOO Institute of Techcowlogy (MIT), has started a startup! Allen is the president of his startup. He also hires n−1n-1n−1 other employees, each of which is assigned a direct superior. If uuu is a superior of vvv and vvv is a superior of www then also uuu is a superior of www.
