ARC072E Alice in linear land < DP >

Posted on 2018-09-25 Symbols count in article: 993 Reading time ≈ 4 mins.

Problem

Alice in linear land

Time  limit:  2  Sec\mathrm{Time\;limit:\;2\;Sec}Timelimit:2Sec
Memory  limit:  256  MB\mathrm{Memory\;limit:\;256\;MB}Memorylimit:256MB

Statement

Alice lives on a line. Today, she will travel to some place in a mysterious vehicle. Initially, the distance between Alice and her destination is DDD. When she input a number xxx to the vehicle, it will travel in the direction of the destination by a distance of xxx if this move would shorten the distance between the vehicle and the destination, and it will stay at its position otherwise. Note that the vehicle may go past the destination when the distance between the vehicle and the destination is less than xxx.
Alice made a list of NNN numbers. The ithi^{th}ith number in this list is did_idi​. She will insert these numbers to the vehicle one by one.
However, a mischievous witch appeared. She is thinking of rewriting one number in the list so that Alice will not reach the destination after NNN moves.
She has QQQ plans to do this, as follows:
Rewrite only the qithq_i^{th}qith​ number in the list with some integer so that Alice will not reach the destination.
Write a program to determine whether each plan is feasible.

1≤N≤5×1051\le N\le5\times10^51≤N≤5×105
1≤Q≤5×1051\le Q\le5\times10^51≤Q≤5×105
1≤D≤1091\le D\le10^91≤D≤109
1≤di≤109  (1≤i≤N)1\le d_i\le10^9\;(1\le i\le N)1≤di​≤109(1≤i≤N)
1≤qi≤N  (1≤i≤Q)1\le q_i\le N\;(1\le i\le Q)1≤qi​≤N(1≤i≤Q)
DDD and each did_idi​ are integers.

Print QQQ lines. The ithi^{th}ith line should contain YES if the ithi^{th}ith plan is feasible, and NO otherwise.

Translation

有一个无限长的数轴，你现在在DDD处，有NNN条指令，每条指令为一个整数did_idi​。顺次处理每条指令，如果你当前在xxx处，你会走到min⁡(x,∣x−di∣)\min(x,|x-d_i|)min(x,∣x−di​∣)处。有QQQ条询问，对于每条询问，回答能否通过改变dqid_{q_i}dqi​​的值使得最后不能到达000。

Solution

精妙的做法，有点类似构造，又有点像DP\mathrm{DP}DP。

首先处理出数组AAA，其中AiA_iAi​表示在没有更改指令的情况下，处理前iii条指令后的位置。那么如果能够修改did_idi​，我们可以在处理完did_idi​后到达[0,Ai−1][0,A_{i-1}][0,Ai−1​]中的所有位置。我们需要找到一个数组BBB，其中BiB_iBi​表示修改并处理完did_idi​后，至少要在哪个位置才能使处理完后面的指令后不能到达000。显然，如果Aq−1≥BqA_{q-1}\ge B_qAq−1​≥Bq​，一定能找到可行方案，否则一定不能。

考虑如何得到BBB。我们倒序计算BBB。首先，显然有Bn=1B_n=1Bn​=1。不难发现BBB一定是递减的，于是倒序计算时我们用前面的值作为基础值来处理出现在处理到的BBB。对于Bi  (i∈[1,n))B_i\;(i\in[1,n))Bi​(i∈[1,n))，BiB_iBi​不小于Bi+1B_{i+1}Bi+1​，如果2Bi+1≤di+12B_{i+1}\le d_{i+1}2Bi+1​≤di+1​，那么从Bi+1B_{i+1}Bi+1​位置进行di+1d_{i+1}di+1​指令一定不会走到更小的点上，因此BiB_iBi​可以取到其最小值Bi+1B_{i+1}Bi+1​；否则，需要使其操作di+1d_{i+1}di+1​后不小于Bi+1B_{i+1}Bi+1​，因此Bi=Bi+1+di+1B_i=B_{i+1}+d_{i+1}Bi​=Bi+1​+di+1​。

处理出A,BA,BA,B后判断即可。时间复杂度O(N+Q)O(N+Q)O(N+Q)。
